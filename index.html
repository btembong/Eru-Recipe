Eru with fufu Recipe

A traditional delicacy from Cameroon

Prep: 15 min Cook: 35 min Serves: 8â€“10

Ingredients

  • Fresh eru leaves (or spinach substitute)
  • Waterleaf (or pumpkin leaves)
  • Assorted meat (beef, goat, or chicken)
  • Smoked fish
  • Cow skin (kanda)
  • Palm oil
  • Crayfish
  • Seasoning cubes and salt
  • Hot pepper (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash and slice eru and waterleaf finely.
  2. Boil meat, cow skin, and fish with seasoning until tender.
  3. Add palm oil and crayfish to the pot.
  4. Mix in waterleaf, then add eru leaves gradually.
  5. Simmer until leaves are soft and flavors blend.
  6. Serve hot with fufu corn, garri, or water fufu.

Notes

Eru, or Okazi leaves (as it's called in Nigeria), is a creeping plant, Gnetum Africanum, that grows wild in the African forest.

Enjoy your meal
